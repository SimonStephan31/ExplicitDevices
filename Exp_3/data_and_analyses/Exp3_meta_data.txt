exp_3_data.csv

Contains the raw data file used in the R script

Columns: 
"i"			(participant ID)
"exp" 			(experimental condition, where 0 is "scrambled", 1 is "mechanism") 
"composition" 		(the three mechanism choices made by participants)
"A"			(participants' explanation for their first choice)
"B"			(participants' explanation or their second choice)
"C"			(participants explanation or their third choice)
"kA"			(list of mentioned dispositions for first component) 
"nA"			(number of mentioned dispositions or this component) 
"mA"			(number of joint dispositions of that component with other components of the present mechanism)
... same logic applies to the other columns

"age"			(participants' age in years)
"gender"		(participants' gender)
