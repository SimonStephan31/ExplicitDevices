Exp1_subj_explanations.csv 

Contains the raw data file that got subjected to the GPT parsing code. 

Columns: 
"condition" 	(experimental condition, where 1 is "functional", 2 is "dysfunctional", and 3 is "separate parts") 
"nummer" 	(participant ID) 
"desktop_conf" 	(participants' response to the question asking them to confirm to take part via Desktop or Laptop)
"attent_conf" 	(participants' response to the question asking them to confirm to take their participation seriously)
"machine_type" 	(experimental condition again, but with labels)
"machine_pic" 	(the stimulus/machine picture that participants were shown)
"mem_check" 	(participants' response to a memory check question asking them to select the object that was not in the stimulus) 
"mem_check_correct" 	(coding whether participants answered the memory check question correctly)
"age"		(age in years) 
"gender"	(gender)
"tech_issues"	(potential technical issues participants may have experienced)

Exp1_parsing_results.csv

Contains the results of the GPT parsing over participants' descriptions. 

Columns: 
"nummer"	(see above)
"machine_type"	(see above)
"model"		(the version of GPT that did the parsing) 
"entities"	(the entities identified in participants' descriptions by GPT) 
"activities" 	(the activities identified in participants' descriptions by GPT)
"arrangements"	(the arrangements identified in participants' descriptions by GPT)
"expl_length" 	(length of participants' descriptions; unit is words) 
"num_ent"	(number of extracted listed entities) 
"num_act"	(number of extracted listed activities) 
"num_arr"	(number of extracted listed arrangements)
